Cung Chow Diner

    Cung Chow Restaurant is Express Chinese cuisine restaurant in San Jose California where you can explore authentic Chinese dishes. We prepare our dishes with fresh and quality ingredients, along with our passion and love of cooking. We always improve our dishes to reach the right taste in each customers, your satisfaction is the reason we stay in business. Our house special dishes include: Fried Wonton, Shish Kabob Chiken - Beef - Pork on Stick, Shrimp Drunken Noodle Spicy, House Special Lo Mein and House Special Fired Rice
